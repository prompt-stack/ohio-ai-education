Strategic Insights & Recommendations

Critical findings from our comprehensive analysis of Ohio's AI education market:

Market Size Revelation

Our research revealed 1,201 eligible employers (not just 651), increasing annual funding potential from $117M to $216M+. This represents a 85% increase in market opportunity.

Trainer Economics

450 trainers generating $5M+ annually in local income creates powerful economic incentives for participation and retention. Each trainer earns $6,000+ annually.

Geographic Strategy

Cincinnati Metro provides ideal pilot region with 4 ESCs, 53 districts, and $24M funding potential. Success here enables statewide scaling.

Implementation Speed

With full employer participation, entire state could be trained in under 1 year from funding perspective. Practical constraints suggest 3-5 year optimal timeline.

Competitive Moats

First-mover advantage with ESC partnerships, state strategy alignment, and local trainer networks creates sustainable competitive advantages.

Scalability Model

ESC network provides built-in distribution infrastructure. 51 ESCs can coordinate 600+ districts more efficiently than direct district relationships.
